Why Twilio SendGrid Transactional Email?

  • 01 Flexible Integration

    Create custom features around the specific email needs of your business via Twilio SendGrid’s simple SMTP relay or flexible Web and SMTP APIs. The APIs enable broad control over email handling, processing, and reporting.

  • 02 Team Empowerment

    Marketers can navigate Twilio SendGrid’s entire feature set through the UI, easily owning their content and monitoring sending metrics. Full-feature parity between Twilio SendGrid UI and Web API means that developers are unbound in what they can accomplish with a few lines of code.

  • 03 Deliverability Partner

    Self-support is also available through extensive documentation, an online knowledge base, and an active online support community.

  • 04 On-Demand Scalability

    With a versatile infrastructure that is built to grow with you, Twilio SendGrid can dynamically adjust as your email volume changes, eliminating the need for you to predict, provision, or maintain mail server capacity.

 

Example Use Cases for Twilio SendGrid Transactional Email

Purchase receipts

After customers sign up, follow up with an email that confirms their order and includes their shipping information and tracking links when applicable.

Password resets

Password reset emails allow users to recover passwords or set new ones in an effort to gain access to your app.

Account verifications

Send confirmation email to verify new subscribers’ account information after they sign up for your emails.

Reactivation Email

Sent to subscribers who have previously interacted with brand, but haven’t continued to engage to keep your brand top-of-mind.

Alerts

Notify or alert your users of important activities going on in your web or mobile app such as account usage alert, update to social posts etc.

Surveys

Requests some form of feedback on a recent transaction they had with your business to understand more about their experience.
